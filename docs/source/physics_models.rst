Physics Models
==============

Z3ST provides a variety of physical models for thermo-mechanical analysis.

Thermal Model
-------------
Solves the heat conduction equation with volumetric heating and Robin-type gap conductance.

Mechanical Model
----------------
Solves linear elasticity and J2 plasticity problems.
- **Differentiable Constitutive Laws**: Custom material laws can be defined as UFL expressions.

Phase-Field Damage
------------------
Implements AT1 and AT2 models for brittle fracture.

Cluster Dynamics
----------------
1D DG solver for defect cluster evolution in size space.
